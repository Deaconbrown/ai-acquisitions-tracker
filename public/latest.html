This week's biggest deal
Schneider Electric acquires Cognite in $3.1 billion deal
Reported by qz.com →

Schneider Electric's substantial acquisition of Cognite signifies a major push into industrial AI, aiming to unify operational and information technology data. This move highlights the growing importance of data infrastructure as the foundation for advanced AI applications in heavy industry. By integrating Cognite's platform, Schneider Electric is positioning itself to offer more comprehensive AI driven solutions for asset performance management and operational efficiency. The deal underscores a trend of large industrial players seeking to leverage AI to unlock significant value from their vast data resources.
