What to watch
This week saw a flurry of acquisitions focused on expanding AI capabilities within specific industries like fintech, cybersecurity, and hospitality. The MoonPay acquisition of Entendre highlights the growing demand for AI-driven financial operations and accounting tools. Meanwhile, the robust funding round for Groq and the advancements in Alibaba's AI video model suggest a continued arms race in foundational AI technology and infrastructure. Expect more targeted acquisitions as companies seek to integrate specialized AI solutions into their existing business models.
