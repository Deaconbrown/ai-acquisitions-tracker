What to watch
This week's acquisitions highlight a clear trend of AI companies seeking to deepen their control over the technological stack. From hardware integration to specialized tooling and talent acquisition, the focus is on building more self-sufficient and performant AI systems. Expect further consolidation as companies aim to combine foundational model development with the underlying infrastructure needed to scale them efficiently. The drive for energy efficiency in AI compute also suggests a growing emphasis on sustainable and cost-effective scaling solutions.
