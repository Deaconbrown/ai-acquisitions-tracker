What to watch
This week's acquisitions demonstrate a clear pattern: companies are aggressively consolidating AI capabilities. We expect this trend to accelerate as companies race to control key components of the AI value chain, driving further M&A activity in infrastructure, specialized tooling, and companies with unique AI expertise.
