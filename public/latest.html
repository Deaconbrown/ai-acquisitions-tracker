What to watch
This week's acquisitions show a clear trend: large tech firms are aggressively acquiring specialized AI talent and capabilities to bolster their existing platforms. Expect continued consolidation, particularly in areas where AI can drive efficiency or unlock new revenue streams, and watch for emerging AI marketplaces as compute and data become more commoditized.
