This move underscores the intense competition in the AI hardware space, where major players are racing to secure next-generation chip technology. By acquiring Modular, Qualcomm aims to integrate advanced AI processing capabilities directly into its mobile and automotive platforms, enhancing performance and efficiency for AI workloads.
