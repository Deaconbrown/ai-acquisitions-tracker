What to watch
This week saw a flurry of AI-related acquisitions spanning diverse sectors, from defense simulation to compliance and grocery intelligence. The most striking trend is the significant investment in energy infrastructure to power AI, exemplified by Elon Musk's substantial acquisitions. This suggests a critical bottleneck is emerging in the AI industry, where compute power demands are driving the acquisition of traditional energy companies. Expect continued consolidation and strategic plays as companies race to secure the necessary resources for large-scale AI deployment.
