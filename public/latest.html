This week's biggest deal
Qualcomm acquires AI startup Modular in open ecosystem bet to challenge CUDA
Reported by SDxCentral →

Qualcomm's acquisition of Modular signals a significant move to democratize AI development, aiming to break Nvidia's dominance in the GPU software ecosystem. By focusing on an open platform, Qualcomm seeks to enable broader adoption of AI across its diverse chipsets, from mobile to automotive. This strategy positions Qualcomm to challenge Nvidia's closed CUDA environment and capture a larger share of the growing AI hardware and software market. The deal underscores the increasing importance of software and ecosystem control in the AI hardware race.

This week's acquisitions highlight a broad trend: established tech giants are aggressively acquiring AI talent and technology to bolster their platforms and challenge competitors.
