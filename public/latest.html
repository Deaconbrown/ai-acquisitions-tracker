This week's biggest deal
Anthropic launches Claude Tag, replacing its Slack app with a persistent AI teammate that learns, monitors and works autonomously
Reported by VentureBeat →

Anthropic's launch of Claude Tag marks a significant shift from single-user chatbots to persistent AI agents embedded within collaboration platforms. This move directly targets the enterprise workflow layer, aiming to become an integrated team member rather than just a tool. The strategic logic is clear: owning the communication hub grants unparalleled access to contextual data and deepens user integration, creating significant switching costs and market advantage. This signals a major push toward AI agents acting as autonomous workers within organizations.

What to watch
This week highlights a clear trend toward AI integration within core enterprise workflows and platforms. Acquisitions in tooling and data are enabling more autonomous AI agents, while hardware players are bolstering their AI capabilities. We expect to see continued consolidation as companies seek to embed AI deeply into their offerings and gain strategic positions within collaboration and communication layers. The focus will remain on AI's ability to move beyond simple task execution to become an integrated, proactive participant in business operations.
