What to watch
This week highlights a bifurcated M&A landscape in AI. Major enterprise players like Salesforce are making significant strategic acquisitions to embed AI into core offerings, while a host of smaller companies are being acquired to bolster specific AI capabilities in areas like security, finance, and AIoT. The influx of capital into AI infrastructure, from data centers to cooling, also signals continued investment in the foundational elements required for widespread AI adoption. Expect more consolidation as companies seek to acquire specialized AI talent and technology to maintain a competitive edge.
