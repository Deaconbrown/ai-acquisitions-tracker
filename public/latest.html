This week's biggest deal
Cohere cracks lossless quantization and native citations with first full Apache 2.0 licensed open model Command A+
Reported by VentureBeat →

Cohere's release of Command A+ marks a significant strategic move in the open-source AI landscape. By offering a powerful, highly optimized model under a permissive Apache 2.0 license, Cohere is betting on the enterprise demand for sovereign AI solutions. This approach directly challenges proprietary models by providing a path for companies to run advanced AI securely within their own environments, fostering trust and adoption, particularly in regulated industries through its native citation feature.
