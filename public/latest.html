What to watch
This week's deal flow shows a continued diversification in AI acquisitions, spanning cybersecurity, fintech, and hardware. The acquisition by Cribl, in particular, underscores the increasing value placed on AI-native capabilities within data infrastructure platforms. Expect to see more vertical-specific AI solutions being integrated into broader enterprise software stacks as companies race to embed intelligence across their operations. The trend towards acquiring specialized AI talent and technology, rather than solely building it in-house, will likely persist.
