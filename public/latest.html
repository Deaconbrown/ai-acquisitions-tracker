This week's biggest deal
Meta unwinds $2 billion Manus acquisition after China order
Reported by qz.com →

The sudden unwinding of Meta's substantial $2 billion acquisition of Manus is a stark reminder of the complex geopolitical forces shaping the AI landscape. This deal's collapse signals that even well-capitalized tech giants are not immune to international regulatory pressures, particularly those involving sensitive technology and China. The strategic logic for Meta was likely to bolster its AI hardware and robotics capabilities, but the need to comply with government directives has overridden its acquisition ambitions. This event underscores the increasing importance of regulatory due diligence in large-scale AI M&A.
