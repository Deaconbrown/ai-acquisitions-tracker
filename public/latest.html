This week's acquisitions reveal a clear strategy of AI companies seeking to solidify their market positions by acquiring specialized capabilities and domain expertise. Expect this pattern to continue as AI providers look to offer more comprehensive, industry specific solutions, moving beyond general AI models to deep application.
