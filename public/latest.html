What to watch
This week’s activity shows a clear trend towards AI integration in core business functions, from rights management in entertainment to customer acquisition in niche markets. The acquisition of Sureel AI by Warner Music Group exemplifies how established industries are seeking AI solutions to manage digital assets and intellectual property. Meanwhile, the data center expansion in South Korea points to continued investment in the physical infrastructure required to support AI's growing demands. Expect continued consolidation as companies look to acquire specialized AI capabilities and the underlying resources to deploy them.
