Exclusive: Apple acquires Xnor.ai, edge AI spin-out from Paul Allen’s AI2, for price in $200M range
Reported by GeekWire →

Apple's reported acquisition of Xnor.ai marks a significant push into on-device AI processing. This move signals a strategic imperative for Apple to enhance its hardware capabilities with advanced machine learning, allowing for more sophisticated features that run locally on its devices. By securing this edge AI technology, Apple aims to strengthen its ecosystem and potentially reduce reliance on cloud-based AI, offering improved privacy and performance to its users.
