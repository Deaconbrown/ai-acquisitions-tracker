Marvell's Celestial AI Acquisition Signals Strategic Push into Photonics
Reported by IDTechEx →

Marvell's acquisition of Celestial AI marks a significant move into the burgeoning photonics sector, driven by the need for high-speed data transfer critical for AI workloads. This strategic integration addresses the bottleneck of data movement within data centers, a growing concern as AI models become more complex. The deal underscores the increasing convergence of semiconductor and optical technologies, signaling a future where AI infrastructure relies heavily on photonics for efficiency and scalability.
