Cloudflare acquires Vite creator VoidZero to accelerate AI-coded app development

Cloudflare's acquisition of VoidZero, the creator of Vite, signals a significant push into accelerating AI-native application development. By integrating Vite's fast build tooling, Cloudflare aims to streamline the process of creating applications that leverage AI. This move underscores the growing importance of developer experience and efficient tooling in the AI ecosystem, suggesting a trend towards optimizing the infrastructure that supports AI innovation.
