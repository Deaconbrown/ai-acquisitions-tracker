What to watch
This week's acquisitions highlight a clear pattern: established companies are buying AI capabilities to integrate into their core businesses, rather than acquiring AI as a standalone product. From building management to IT services and neurostimulation, the focus is on enhancing existing offerings. Expect this trend to continue as companies across sectors seek to leverage AI for operational efficiency and advanced product features. The key will be how seamlessly these integrations translate into tangible value for end users and shareholders.
