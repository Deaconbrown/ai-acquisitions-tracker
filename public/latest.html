Salesforce acquires AI customer service co Fin for $3.6B

Salesforce's acquisition of AI customer service startup Fin for $3.6 billion signals a clear push into generative AI for enterprise customer interactions. This move demonstrates a strategic intent to integrate advanced AI capabilities directly into its core CRM offerings, enhancing customer support and sales processes. The substantial price tag reflects the perceived value of AI-powered solutions in a competitive market, underscoring the race among major tech players to dominate the AI-driven customer experience landscape.

What to watch
