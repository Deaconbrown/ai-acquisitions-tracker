What to watch
This week's deals show a strong trend of established companies acquiring AI capabilities to enhance their core offerings, particularly in tooling and infrastructure. We will continue to watch for companies looking to integrate AI for efficiency gains and for startups that offer specialized AI solutions, as the market consolidates and larger players aim to build comprehensive AI stacks.
