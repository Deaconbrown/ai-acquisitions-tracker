Analog Devices Expands AI Power Strategy with $1.5B Empower Acquisition
Reported by Insider Monkey →

Analog Devices' acquisition of Empower for $1.5 billion underscores the critical intersection of AI and power management. As AI models grow more complex, the demand for efficient, high-performance power solutions escalates. This move signals a strategic bet on the foundational infrastructure required for widespread AI deployment, particularly in data centers and edge computing. The integration of Empower's technology will likely enable Analog Devices to offer more comprehensive solutions, optimizing AI performance and energy consumption.
