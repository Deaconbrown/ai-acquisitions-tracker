What to watch
This week’s acquisitions highlight a trend of established companies integrating AI capabilities and AI startups seeking strategic partnerships. The massive valuation of the potential SpaceX-Anthropic deal underscores the premium commanded by leading AI research firms. Expect continued consolidation as companies across various sectors seek to bolster their AI offerings, whether through direct acquisition or strategic stakes.
