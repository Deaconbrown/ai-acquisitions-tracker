What to watch
This week's AI acquisition landscape shows a clear trend towards strategic integration rather than pure R&D plays. Companies are buying AI capabilities to enhance existing products and services, particularly in manufacturing, cybersecurity, and developer tools. The potential SpaceX acquisition of Cursor highlights the burgeoning value of AI-native development platforms. Expect continued focus on AI startups that offer tangible productivity gains or integrate seamlessly into enterprise workflows, particularly as large tech players solidify their AI infrastructure and tooling plays.
