What to watch
This week saw significant activity in AI acquisitions, ranging from infrastructure plays like Rumble's acquisition of Northern Data to tooling companies such as DeductiveAI and Cursor. The high valuations, particularly in the software and tooling sectors, indicate continued investor confidence in AI's commercial potential. Expect more strategic acquisitions as companies seek to integrate AI capabilities rapidly, focusing on both foundational technology and application-specific tools. The trend also highlights an increasing interest in companies that can accelerate development cycles and improve operational efficiency.
