What to watch
This week’s acquisitions show a clear trend: companies are rapidly integrating AI across various functions, from cybersecurity and data collaboration to talent acquisition. The sheer volume of deals, including substantial ones like Diginex's $1.5 billion announcement, indicates that M&A remains a primary strategy for companies seeking to rapidly deploy AI capabilities. We anticipate this activity will continue, focusing on companies that offer specialized AI solutions or significant market advantages, particularly in areas like data infrastructure and specialized AI tooling.
