This week's biggest deal
Apple buys secretive Israeli AI company Q.ai for $1.6 billion in biggest acquisition of its history
Reported by The Jerusalem Post →

Apple's blockbuster $1.6 billion acquisition of Q.ai marks a significant strategic move into advanced AI capabilities. The secretive nature of the Israeli startup suggests Apple is focusing on foundational AI research, likely aimed at bolstering its long-term product roadmap and competitive edge against rivals investing heavily in generative AI. This deal signals Apple's intent to deepen its AI integration across its ecosystem, from core operating systems to its expanding services.
