BARBRI Acquires Lega, Completing the Startup's Pivot From AI Governance to Experiential AI Training
Reported by LawSites →

This acquisition by BARBRI, a legal education giant, of Lega, an AI training startup, signals a significant shift in professional development. The legal sector, often seen as slow to adopt new technologies, is now actively integrating AI. BARBRI's move suggests a strategic pivot towards providing practical, hands-on AI skills, moving beyond theoretical governance to real-world application for law students and professionals. This acquisition highlights the growing demand for AI fluency across all industries.
