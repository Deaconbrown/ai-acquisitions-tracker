What to watch
This week's acquisitions highlight a strong trend in integrating AI across various sectors, from defense and security to construction and employee experience. Companies are actively seeking AI capabilities to enhance their existing platforms and services. The proliferation of AI in specialized industries, such as construction compliance and application security, suggests a maturing market where AI is no longer a standalone technology but a critical component for innovation and competitive advantage. Expect continued consolidation as companies race to embed AI into their core offerings.
