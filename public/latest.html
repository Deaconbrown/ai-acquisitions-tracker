What to watch
This week's acquisitions show a clear trend: companies are building out AI capabilities by integrating data infrastructure and specialized tooling. Large enterprises like SAP are buying into companies that enhance data analysis for AI. Simultaneously, AI-native firms are expanding their agent functionalities. We expect to see continued consolidation in the AI infrastructure and tooling sectors as companies race to offer comprehensive AI solutions. Geopolitical considerations, as highlighted by the China story, will also increasingly influence deal-making and market strategies.
