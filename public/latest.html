Google's acquisition of Wiz signals a major push into cloud security, specifically leveraging AI for threat detection and compliance. This move addresses the growing complexity of cloud environments and the need for sophisticated, AI-driven security solutions. The strategic logic is clear: integrate Wiz's expertise to enhance Google Cloud's security offerings, making its platform more attractive to enterprises concerned about cloud vulnerabilities.
