SpaceX Acquires AI Coding Star Cursor for $60 Billion

It suggests that established tech giants are willing to pay a premium to integrate cutting-edge AI capabilities directly into their core operations, rather than build them independently. The rapid ascent of Cursor, reportedly built from communities like Discord, highlights a new paradigm for uncovering and scaling innovative AI talent.
