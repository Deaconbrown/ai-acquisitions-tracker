Elon Musk Acquires APR Energy in Deal Valued at More Than $1 Billion
Reported by TechRepublic →

Elon Musk's acquisition of APR Energy for over $1 billion signals a significant strategic move to secure massive energy infrastructure for his AI ventures, particularly xAI. This deal underscores the growing demand for reliable and scalable power sources necessary to fuel the computationally intensive world of artificial intelligence. The logic is clear: AI development requires substantial energy, and controlling that supply chain is paramount for future growth and innovation.
