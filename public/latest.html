Qualcomm Acquires Modular for $3.9 Billion to Bolster AI Capabilities
Reported by GuruFocus →

Qualcomm's substantial $3.9 billion acquisition of Modular signals a significant strategic push into AI infrastructure. This move aims to enhance Qualcomm's AI software stack, particularly for edge and cloud deployments, and underscores the company's commitment to on-device AI and inference. The deal also highlights the increasing value of specialized AI software platforms in enabling complex AI workloads.
