What to watch
This week saw significant consolidation in AI, with a strong emphasis on tooling and infrastructure. The sheer scale of the reported SpaceX acquisition of Cursor points to a trend of major players seeking to internalize critical AI capabilities. We expect to see continued M&A activity focused on companies that provide foundational AI development tools, enhance AI operational efficiency, or offer unique AI models. The race for AI dominance is driving aggressive moves by established companies to secure both talent and technology.
