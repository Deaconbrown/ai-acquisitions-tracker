This week's biggest deal
Cribl acquires Israeli cyber startup CardinalOps for around $100 million
Reported by calcalistech.com →

Cribl's acquisition of CardinalOps for $100 million signals a growing trend: established companies bolstering their security offerings with AI capabilities. This move allows Cribl to integrate AI-driven detection engineering directly into its data management platform. The strategic logic is clear, enabling faster threat identification and response for customers navigating increasingly complex cyber landscapes. This acquisition underscores the market's demand for AI-powered security solutions.
