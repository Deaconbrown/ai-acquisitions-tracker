What to watch
This week's acquisitions highlight the increasing fragmentation of the AI landscape and the subsequent drive for consolidation. Companies are acquiring specialized AI capabilities to integrate into broader platforms, from live commerce discovery to healthcare decision intelligence and enterprise development tools. The focus remains on practical application and the development lifecycle, suggesting that the next wave of AI innovation will prioritize robust, reliable, and integrated solutions over standalone capabilities.
