Asana's acquisition of StackAI signals a clear strategic move to embed AI agent orchestration directly into workflow management. The deal highlights the growing demand for platforms that can manage complex AI deployments, pushing beyond simple automation to sophisticated interaction across enterprise systems.

What to watch
This week's deal flow shows a significant focus on integrating AI capabilities into existing productivity and security platforms. Expect continued M&A activity as companies rush to embed AI across their product suites and protect their AI investments.
