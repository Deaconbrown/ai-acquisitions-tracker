What to watch
This week's acquisitions highlight a clear pattern: companies are integrating AI to enhance existing platforms, particularly in customer service, enterprise software, and specialized industry tools. We anticipate continued consolidation as larger players seek to acquire specialized AI talent and technology to remain competitive, with a focus on practical applications rather than pure research.
