This week's biggest deal
Analog Devices to Acquire AI-focused Empower Semiconductor in a $1.5 billion Deal
Reported by Tekedia →

The $1.5 billion acquisition of Empower Semiconductor by Analog Devices signals a significant move towards integrating specialized AI processing capabilities at the hardware level. This deal underscores the growing demand for efficient, on-chip AI acceleration, particularly in areas like edge computing and autonomous systems. Analog Devices is clearly aiming to bolster its portfolio with dedicated silicon solutions that can handle the immense computational needs of modern AI, moving beyond general-purpose processors.

What to watch
This week's acquisitions highlight a clear trend: companies are aggressively acquiring AI capabilities, whether for specialized hardware, enhanced software platforms, or robust data security. We expect this surge to continue, with particular attention on companies that can bolster AI's efficiency, security, and deployment across diverse industries.
