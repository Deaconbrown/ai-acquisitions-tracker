This week's biggest deal
Ecolab Closes CoolIT $4.75bn Acquisition to Scale AI Cooling
Reported by Data Centre Magazine →

Ecolab's substantial $4.75 billion acquisition of CoolIT signals a major strategic move into AI-driven cooling solutions. This deal highlights the increasing importance of specialized infrastructure needed to support the immense power demands of artificial intelligence. The acquisition aims to leverage CoolIT's expertise to scale solutions, addressing a critical bottleneck in the rapid expansion of AI compute capacity. It demonstrates a clear trend of established players acquiring niche technology to integrate AI capabilities directly into their core offerings.

What to watch
This week's deals show a clear pattern of companies seeking to integrate AI capabilities, whether through direct acquisition or by building out essential infrastructure. From cybersecurity and compliance to data center cooling and revenue management, AI is becoming a core driver of strategic M&A across various sectors. We expect to see continued focus on companies that provide foundational AI services or enable its broader deployment, particularly in areas facing significant technical or regulatory hurdles.
