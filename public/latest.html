Dassault Systemes Acquires ArisGlobal to Boost AI in Life Sciences
Reported by ET Manufacturing →

Dassault Systemes' acquisition of ArisGlobal signals a significant move to embed AI capabilities within its life sciences solutions. This strategic acquisition aims to enhance drug discovery and development processes, a critical area for AI innovation. The deal highlights a broader trend of established enterprise software companies acquiring specialized AI firms to accelerate their digital transformation and maintain a competitive edge. By integrating ArisGlobal's AI expertise, Dassault Systemes is positioning itself to offer more intelligent, data-driven platforms to its clients in a rapidly evolving sector.
