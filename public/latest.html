Cribl acquires Israeli cyber startup CardinalOps for around $100 million
Reported by calcalistech.com →

Cribl's acquisition of CardinalOps for approximately $100 million highlights the growing demand for AI-driven cybersecurity solutions. This move signals a consolidation trend where companies are seeking to enhance their security postures with advanced AI capabilities. The strategic logic for Cribl is clear: integrating CardinalOps' threat detection and analysis into its platform will offer customers more robust, intelligent security operations, a critical need in today's threat landscape.
